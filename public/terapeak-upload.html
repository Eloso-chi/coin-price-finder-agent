How to export from Terapeak

  1. Go to eBay Seller Hub > Research
  2. Search for a coin (e.g. "1878-CC Morgan Silver Dollar")
  3. Make sure the Sold tab is selected
  4. Scroll down to the sold listings table
  5. Click the Download or Export button
  6. Save the CSV, then drag it here
  7. Repeat for each coin -- you can drop multiple files at once!
